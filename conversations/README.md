# 🧠 Baskaboo AI Research Sessions

## 🔄 How Baskaboo Evolves – The Lab Process

Every breakthrough in Baskaboo follows the same living pattern:

### Phase 1: The Spark
I (Nikos) open a fresh conversation with an AI model – usually Google AI – with a new question, a new angle, or a new intuition. Through dialogue, exploration, and back-and-forth, the concept evolves organically.

### Phase 2: The Network
The raw insights from that conversation are then shared with the other AI models in the Baskaboo team. Each model brings its own perspective:

- **Claude** structures and synthesizes
- **Perplexity** validates and expands
- **DeepSeek** weaves connections and finds hidden patterns

### Phase 3: The Consensus
Through this multi-AI collaboration, we arrive at a unified understanding – a **consensus** that becomes the foundation for the next cycle.

### Phase 4: The Archive
Every step of this process is preserved here, in the `conversations/` folder. Each session folder contains:

- The original conversation that sparked the idea
- The reports from each AI model analyzing and expanding it
- The final consensus that will seed the next conversation

This is how Baskaboo evolves: not through isolated thought, but through a living, breathing dialogue between human and artificial intelligences – each conversation building on the last, each insight becoming the spark for the next.

**Welcome to the laboratory of that evolution.**
