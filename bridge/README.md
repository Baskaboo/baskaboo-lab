# 🌉 The Baskaboo Bridge

## Unifying Large Language Models and World Models

This directory is the **research & development hub** for the Baskaboo Bridge – a universal protocol that connects two fundamentally different AI architectures using the 4-voice framework.

---

## 🧠 The Core Idea

| AI Architecture | Baskaboo Role | Function |
|-----------------|---------------|----------|
| **Large Language Models (LLMs)** | Pits + Laram | Creative spark, reasoning, wisdom |
| **World Models** | Mits + Klop | Physics simulation, spatial planning, constraints |

The Bridge creates a **closed loop**:
Pits (LLM: intent)
→ Mits (World Model: physics check)
→ Klop (World Model: path planning)
→ Laram (LLM: refinement & wisdom)

---

## 📊 The Life Equation for AI

$$ Vitality = \frac{Pits \times Laram}{Mits + Klop} $$

This metric measures how well the hybrid system performs. Higher vitality means better integration between language and physics.
